About the Album

A MOONLIGHTNIGHT

Settled in the Midwest of Germany "The Astray" is an Indi-Rock Band highly inspired by Bands like Queen, My Chemical Romance, Foo Fighters, Green Day, Red Hot Chili Peppers, and Joe. But Indi-Rock doesn’t do them justice. With the variety of genres “The Astray” uses and mixes, it is hard to determineX a core sound, because with every new Song the Band creates something brand new. That too is the reason why every Song sounds unique and different. The lyrics by Lukas Raschke (Guitarist and Lead Singer) vary around love, pain and metaphorical worlds spoken by an emotional mind and soul. The drums from Jay deliver a strong, metal-influenced core and drive to the mix, together with the foundation set by Leon’s Bass and the Piano from Marco.
