About the Album

A MOONLIGHTNIGHT

The album resembles drifting away into sleep and every song is a different dream. Its about sleeping at the edge of dreams and nightmares between melancolic romanticism, reflecting the past and the tales of the dark. With songs that capture the emotional depths of falling in love, trying to stand up after getting thrown to the ground and learning from lessons of the past.

Songs

  • Welcome to the Jungle

    [...]
    Welcome to the circus
    Welcome to your new home
    They got everything you needed
    but you didn't even know

    Welcome to the Jungle is a metaphor for growing up into our perverted society. The song is about breaking the patterns of a perverted/sick reality, breaking out of one’s cage and letting the “animal” out, so everyone can see what they are really dealing with. It's a song about one’s true nature, about emancipation,
    about showing what is deep down inside of us.

    It's about our deepest - and in this case - darkest desires.
